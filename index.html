Mitchell Naylor

Bio

Mitchell Naylor is an applied machine learning professional with practical experience in natural language processing (NLP), statistical modeling, and deep learning. Mitch currently works as a Senior Data Scientist at Azra AI, where he develops, improves, and deploys clinical NLP models. Additionally, Mitch leads applied research efforts in areas including transfer learning, language modeling, efficient self-attention, and model fairness.

Mitch lives in Nashville, TN. Outside of work, he enjoys rock climbing, hiking, music, and traveling.

  • Methods: NLP, language modeling, deep learning, Bayesian statistics, causal inference
  • Libraries: PyTorch, Hugging Face (Transformers, Tokenizers, Datasets), scikit-learn, XGBoost, spaCy
